Vision and Imaging Science

This aspect of computer science has everything to do with analyzing images using complex algorithms for various purposes. Discover the most important applications and projects below

Face Recognition

A software that takes a picture and compares it to an original picture that has been taken previously. It uses special pattern recognition steps to check whether the person in both pictures is the same one. Uses machine learning to constantly improve the correctnesss rate

Learn more »

Medical Imaging

A program that recognises different organs of humans in a picture, which is used to significantly improve the accuracy of the surgery procedure and the detection of tiny things in a scan picture of bones or skin that the doctor uses to discover disease symptoms.

Learn more »

Machine Vision

With all the material handling, material removal and automated quality control applications out there today, precision and accuracy are necessary for standardization and repeatability. That is where vision comes into play, either as a stand-alone system or integrated with robotics.
